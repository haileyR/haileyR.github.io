Enumerable Methods: map

2014. 10. 01

Enumerable#map

map gives a new array of whatever is returned in a block.

Examples

  • Capitalize words in an array: 
    words = ['monday', 'tuesday', 'wednesday']
words.map {|word| word.capitalize}
    will return ['Monday', 'Tuesday', 'Wednesday'].
  • Do some math: 
    numbers = [1,2,3,4,5,]
numbers.map {|n| n*2}
    will return [2,4,6,8,10].
